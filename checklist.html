Registration checklist

Here's everything you need to start booking shifts with “the good care people”

Nurses
  • Nursing Registration (Nurses & Midwives only)

If you’re a nurse we require your Registration so we can check your registration with the Medical Counsil database.

  • Minimum Two references

We need to collect at least two professional references for your application, so make sure you have at least 2 people that could act as your referee. We accept references from:

Any previous employer from your resume

  • Proof of Identity (Passport or Adhar Card)

To confirm your Proof of Identity, we’ll need a copy of your passport or Adhar Card

  • Police Clearance Certificate
    To confirm your right to work for Clients on behalf of the good care people, Police Clearance is conducted in respect of any person who has access to, or contact with, children or vulnerable persons. As part of our onboarding we required an NOC from your local police station
    To obtain a Non-Involvement in Offence Certificate (NOC) for employment with The Good Care People, download the THUNA app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store. After registering and creating a user ID, navigate to the "Certificates" section within the app and apply for the NOC by completing the required form. You can track your application's status within the app. Once approved, download the certificate and include it with your job application. For assistance, refer to the app's help section or contact Kerala Police customer support.
  • Recent proof of address (3 - 6 months)
  • Copy of CV
  • 2/3 names & contact details of referees
  • Certificates from Community Health Centre on:
    • Manual Handling and People Lifting
    • CPR/BLS
    • Infection Control
    • Hand Hygiene
    • Medication Management
    • Safeguarding Certificates (Adult & Child)
    • ACLS & BLS (If applicable)
  • Fitness to Work Certificate from a general practitioner.
  • Immunity certifications in case of any recent medical conditions.
  • At least one year of work experience
    You’ll need at least one year of experience to find work through the good care people. The clients that use the good care people require that you explain any gaps in your employment history.
  • Copy of any additional Nursing Certificates i.e. ICU, A & E, IV Policy
